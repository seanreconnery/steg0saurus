However, after a while, I became a go-to person when it came to some of these ARG communities and truth seekers due to stumbling on what appears to be James Comey using steganography to covertly communicate on his twitter account as well as a possible embedded PGP encryption key inside a Jeffrey Epstein image from the NYPost.

About

What is STEGANOGRAPHY?

No, it's not a dinosaur type..

Steganography is an ancient word that translate to something along the lines of "hidden writing". It is simply the act of hiding something in plain sight. The technique is as old as time and the processes are numerous. In the digital age, steganography is generally referred to in regards to image or audio steganography--the act of embedding or hiding a secret message inside the data of an image or soundfile. In the grand scheme of things, data is just data--whether that data represents a song or a picture, it's still (at its absolute core) a bunch of 1s and 0s.

About

How Does Steganography Work?

There are numerous methods to conceal data inside an image, but the technique utilized here is referred to as LSB, or Least Significant Bit. In every byte of data, there are 8 bits. These bits hold information about the image--its color pallete, its size, etc.
However, not all bits were created equal and sometimes there are redundant bits of data.
The LSB technique for streganographically hiding information will utilize these least significant bits that contain redudnant data. This is where our secret message will be hidden.

The program goes thru the image, finds the spots it can store extra data, and then bit by bit, it creates a new image that looks virtually identical to the original, except it contains additional information in the form of the hidden message.
